Camping

Please come camp with us in community August 1-5 or any part thereof--we have reserved two large group campsites (Steelhead and Chinook) at Milo McIver State Park with a dedicated family camping area for friends with children.

Milo McIver State Park is located in Estacada, Oregon (just southeast of Portland on the Clackamas River), with room for everyone. The campsites are in a secluded area attached to a huge meadow with volleyball courts and a trail down to the river. Camping will be car-camping style--the campsites are drive-up, and have picnic tables, fire pits, and running water. There are grocery stores 10 minutes away in Estacada, and many more 20-30 minutes away in Portland. If you’re not the cooking-outside type, there are plentiful eateries within a short drive. Hot showers (coin-operated) are located 200 yards from the group campsites.

Guests are encouraged to collaborate and share group meals and equipment and participate in making an awesome community experience.

If staying elsewhere, we invite you to come hang out during the day.

Activities

Milo McIver State Park has one of the finest disc golf courses in the area, and river tubing on the Clackamas River is within a 10-minute drive. There are hiking, biking, and lounging opportunities galore within the park and even more within an hour’s drive (including hot springs and waterfalls--let us know what you’re interested in and we can point you in the right direction).

PLEASE NOTE: The Clackamas River can have very strong currents! Please only swim and tube in safe, designated areas, and watch children closely around river access areas.
